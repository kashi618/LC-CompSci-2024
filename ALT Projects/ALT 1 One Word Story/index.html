One Word Story - Neil Jiang

Welcome to my ALT 1 on Interactive Information Systems

This website is allow users from all over the world to collaborate and make a story. The aim is to tell a coherent story, one word at a time.
> Each player is allowed to input 1 word, and add to the story.
> There is a 10 minute interval if you want to add more words. (This is to prevent spam)
> Existing stories are archived after 24 hours, and a new story will begin.

Rules:

+ Rules from External Website
+ No Slurs or Profanity
+ No Sexual Content keep it clean
+ Do not add unneccesary words or ruin existing stories
+ Do not add personal information
+ Have fun
